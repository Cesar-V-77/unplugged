Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

The retreat is located in the heart of Estes Park, Colorado, just 70 miles northwest of Denver. Guests typically fly into Denver International Airport (DIA) and then enjoy a scenic 90-minute drive through the foothills and the entrance to the Rocky Mountains. Shuttle services, private transfers, or rental cars make the journey easy, and the views along the way are part of the retreat experience.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The Unplugged retreat is ideal for any professional team looking to strengthen bonds, improve communication, or recharge their collective energy. Whether you’re a leadership group seeking strategic clarity, a creative team needing fresh inspiration, or a fast-growing company aligning culture, Unplugged provides the setting and structure to elevate your work relationships. If your team is ready to step away from routine and invest in each other, you’re the right fit.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Unplugged is designed to host teams both small and mid-sized comfortably. With 12 private ensuite rooms, 2 large group meeting rooms, and 4 breakout rooms, we can easily accommodate teams ranging from 8 to about 24 participants. The setup allows space for both group collaboration and personal reflection, so your team can balance productivity with relaxation.

Is there really no wifi available?

Yes — by design, Unplugged retreats minimize digital interruptions to help your team fully disconnect. However, we understand that sometimes connectivity is necessary, which is why we provide a dedicated computer room for moments when wifi access is essential. Outside of that space, you’ll find the absence of constant connectivity allows for deeper conversations, stronger teamwork, and a true reset from daily distractions.
