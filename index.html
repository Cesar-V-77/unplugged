LaCrue Bottling Co. October 2025

In October 2025, LaCrue Bottling Co. spent five incredible days at the Unplugged retreat, and the experience transformed the way their team connects and works together. The historic hotel provided the perfect backdrop — filled with character, comfort, and thoughtful amenities that made every moment feel special. Between meetings, the team explored the scenic lake just half a mile away, where laughter and lighthearted challenges sparked stronger bonds. A full day in the nearby Rocky Mountain National Park gave them the adventure they needed to reset, recharge, and rediscover what teamwork really means. With delicious meals from a private chef team, guided facilitation for daily activities, and peaceful surroundings free from constant notifications, LaCrue Bottling Co. returned to work with more focus, unity, and energy than ever before. Retreat from the desk, return refreshed — just like they did.
